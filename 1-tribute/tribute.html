Cecilia Payne-Gaposchkin

The Woman Who Explanined the Stars

  • May 10, 1900 - Born in Wendover, Buckinghamshire, England
  • 1925 - First person to earn a PhD in astronomy from Radcliffe College of Harvard University. Her thesis showed that the sun was predominantly hydrogen.
  • 1956 - First woman to be promoted to full professor from within the faculty at Harvard's Faculty of Arts and Sciences
  • 1976 - Awarded Henry Norris Russell Prize
  • December 7, 1979 - Died in Cambridge, Massachusetts, U.S.
