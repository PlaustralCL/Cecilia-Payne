Rusell Harmonicas

Product Information

Chromatic Harmonicas

Most chromatic harmonicas have a button-activated lever that directs air to two separate reed plates that include all the notes in the 12-tone western scale.

Diatonic Harmonicas

The most common type of harmonica you’ll encounter in pop, blues and folk music—diatonic harmonicas are designed to play in a specific key.

Tremolo Harmonicas

Tremolo harmonicas produce their distinctive warbling sound through the use of two reeds per note, one tuned slightly sharp, the other slightly flat.





About Rusell Harmonicas

Rusell Harmonicas has been a Dallas tradition since 1910 when it was founded by Mark Rusell. Mark had a passion for music and was determined to make the best harmonicas possible. After a small loan from his best friend Brad Smith, Mark was able to open his harmonica factory. Since that humble beginning, Rusell Harmonicas has been the top manufacture of quality harmonicas in the United States.
